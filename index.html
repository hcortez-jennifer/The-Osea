Get Your Osea Explorer Passport

Every great explorer needs a way to track their journey! The Explorer Passport is a hands-on adventure tool given to every child at the museum entrance. It turns the museum visit into an immersive quest, where each exhibit becomes a mission and you'll get exclusive rewards!

How it Works

Step 1

Pick up your passport at the welcome desk

Step 2

Explore each zone and learn about the amazing sea creatures

Step 3

Stamp your page when you visit a featured exhibition

Step 4

Claim your reward at the end of your visit!
